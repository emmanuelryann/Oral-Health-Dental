Dr. Richard Wyne

Why dentistry?

I’m a dentist because it has always been my desire to help people in a healthcare environment. Growing up in my dad’s and uncle’s dental office, I learned early on that dentistry was going to be my path. I love the relationships we build with our patients and our ability to help them live healthier, better lives.

Dr. Damian Black

Why dentistry?

I chose to pursue dentistry after working alongside my dad and other community health clinics that showed me a path that allowed hands-on creativity and deeper patient relationships. I witnessed how incredible the opportunity was to help someone out of pain, eat more comfortably, smile more confidently, and reclaim their health. One of my favorite things is to transform what can be a “high-fear” environment into a space where people laugh, ask questions, and feel truly cared for.

Education

  • BS in Biology, Canisius College (Buffalo, NY)
  • DDS, University at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine
  • Advanced Education in General Dentistry Residency program, Virginia Commonwealth University
